Write-in (grid-in) math questions — how to enter answers:
  • Enter only one answer, even if you find more than one.
  • Max 5 characters — or 6 if it starts with a negative sign.
  • Fractions are fine if they fit (e.g. 7/2). If a fraction doesn't fit, enter its decimal instead.
  • If a decimal is too long, fill all 5 characters by truncating or rounding the last digit: 2/3 becomes .6666, .6667, 0.666, or 0.667 — never .66 or 0.67.
  • Mixed numbers: enter 3½ as 7/2 or 3.5 (never 31/2).
  • No symbols: %, $, commas, or spaces.
